Andy Hovey

Product strategy and design from Aotearoa New Zealand.

I work on digital products and services. I believe complex challenges are best tackled by diverse and collaborative multidisciplinary teams. I think these teams produce the best answers by osciliating between working to better understand the problem, and making, testing and improving potential solutions.

Since August 2019 I’ve been working with the inspiring team of scientists, engineers and communication experts at Xerra Earth Observation Institute.

In previous design leadership roles I’ve worked on websites and apps for Xero, New Zealand Red Cross, Klim Type Foundry, Kiwibank, New Zealand Arts Festival, Amazon and many others.
