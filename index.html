Product strategy and design from Aotearoa New Zealand

I help organisations build digital products and services – mostly web and mobile apps. I help work out who they’re for, what problems they solve and how they’re designed. On a good day these apps delight the people who use them and grow businesses.

I believe good solutions to complex challenges are most often found when tackled by collaborative and empathetic multidisciplinary teams. These teams follow a process that continually works to better understand the problem, whilst making, testing and improving potential solutions.

I'm currently the head of product and design at Xerra Earth Observation Institute. In previous roles I've been a designer and a leader of small and large teams. I’ve worked on websites and apps for Xero, New Zealand Red Cross, Klim Type Foundry, Kiwibank, New Zealand Arts Festival, Amazon and many others.

Xerra
2019 — Present

Since August 2019 I’ve been working with the inspiring team of scientists, engineers and communication experts at Xerra to develop Starboard, a maritime domain awareness platform.

Starboard marries advanced data science with an intuitive web-based interface to help governments tackle issues ranging from illegal fishing to biosecurity threats presented by international vessel arrivals.
