Design and product leadership

For two decades I’ve been researching, designing, building, and scaling award-winning digital products and services that millions of people around the globe use everyday. I’ve held roles as a designer, creative director, head of design and chief product officer. I’ve led small and large teams at digital agencies, start-ups, and large companies including Amazon, Xero and Starboard.

I believe great software and strong culture comes from collaborative and empathetic multidisciplinary teams who are close to both customer challenges and business opportunities.

Outside work I’m often found slowly suffering up hills on my bike to explore the world, or train for an ultra-endurance bikepacking race.

Starboard
2019 — 2024

Starboard is world leading maritime domain awareness software. It helps governments and organisations reduce illegal fishing and transnational crime, and protect offshore assets.

It’s a sophisticated geospatial platform that elegantly combines 30+ data sources and ~4,500 streaming data points a second.

As Chief Product Officer I’ve been growing the company over the last 5 years. We have an incredible team with a close-knit and productive culture that frequently ships software customers love and use every day.
