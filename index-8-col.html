Product strategy and design from Aotearoa New Zealand.

I use design research and interaction design to help organisations work out which problems to solve and how to solve them. I believe complex challenges are best tackled by collaborative, multidisciplinary teams.

Since August 2019 I’ve been working with the inspiring team of scientists, engineers and communication experts at Xerra Earth Observation Institute. We’re helping monitor Aotearoa’s maritime domain through turning multiple sources of complex satellite data into intuitive and insightful software for analysts, amongst other projects focused on improving our natural environment.

In previous design leadership roles I’ve worked on websites and apps for Xero, New Zealand Red Cross, Klim Type Foundary, Kiwibank, New Zealand Arts Festival, Amazon and many others.
