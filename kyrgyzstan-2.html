Kyrgyzstan

July 3 —
August 7 2018

Decaying soviet architecture contrasts with a resurgent nomadic culture as familes and animals return to yurts each summer. Vodka is downed in corner stores opposite mosques sitting at the feet of jaggy, snow capped mountains.

Warm people smile and kids hi-five cyclists zooming by on their way to idyllic single and double track trails that criss-cross this special place.
